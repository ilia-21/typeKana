Wtf is this?

This is a simple tool I started on a night from 2025-07-15 to 2025-07-16 to simplify learning hiragana

Cool, how do I use it?

Select rows of letters you want to practice and hit start. You'll see hiragana/katakana letters and your job is to write romanji for that letter

Any fancy keybinds?

Of course! On the main page you can hit letter corresponding to group to toggle it. For example hit a, k, s and t to select first 4 rows of characters. For combined characters (like きゃ) use shift + first character, in this case Shift+K. On the test page hit space after typing romanji to accept and go to the next character. Look for text in square brackets like [Esc] to find more keybinds

Anything else?

Check mods (F2)! They make you guess in a diffrent ways. For example Zen just never stops and Time Trials limits the amount of time you have to write the character.

How to close this window?

Every page can be closed on [Esc]

Important info about stats

typeKana ain't damn sweaty competitve platform. It's made to help YOU learn alphabets, so stats here just so YOU see your progress and how much YOU improved. They are also easy to manipulate. All stats and profile info are stored locally in your browser.

Another thing to note is all stats are normalized, so you can't make all characters green. The only way to do this is to REALLY screw up one character, so all other ones will look good.

Also remember that this tool is still in beta so expect stats to break and change values on updates.
