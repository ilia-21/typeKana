Wtf is this?

This is a simple tool I created on night from 2025-07-15 to 2025-07-16 to simplify learning hiragana

Cool, how do I use it?

Select rows of letters you want to practice and hit start. You'll see hiragana/katakana letters and your job is to write romanji for that letter

Any fancy keybinds?

Of course! On the main page you can hit letter corresponding to group to toggle it. For example hit a, k, s and t to select first 4 rows of characters. On the test page hit space after typing romanji to accept and go to the next character. Hit backspace to go to the previous character. (use Ctrl+A to erase, it's 2025!)

How to close this window?

Esc
